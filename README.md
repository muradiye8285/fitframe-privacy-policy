## Privacy Policy

### Overview
FitFrame is designed with a privacy-first approach.  
Image processing is performed on-device.

### Data Collection
FitFrame does not collect personal data.

### Photos Permission
FitFrame requests access to your photo library only to:
- Import photos you choose
- Save exported images back to your library

### Tracking
FitFrame does not track users across apps or websites.

### Third-Party Services
FitFrame does not include third-party advertising SDKs.  
FitFrame does not use third-party analytics SDKs.

### Data Retention
FitFrame does not upload or store your photos on external servers.

### Children’s Privacy
FitFrame does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

### Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated when app features or privacy practices change.
