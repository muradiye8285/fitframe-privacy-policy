Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

FitFrame is an iPhone photo utility that helps users resize and frame photos for different social media aspect ratios. We respect your privacy and keep the app workflow focused on local photo editing.

Data collection

FitFrame does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data. The app does not require account creation and does not use third-party tracking SDKs for user profiling.

Photo access

FitFrame requests access to your photo library only so you can import images you choose and save exported results back to Photos.

On-device processing

Image framing, background generation, zoom, positioning, and export rendering are performed on-device as part of the app workflow.

Network usage

FitFrame’s core editing workflow does not require account login or cloud storage. The app is designed around local editing and export.

Saved exports

When you export an image, FitFrame creates a new processed version and saves it to your photo library if you grant permission. Your original photo remains unchanged.

Children’s privacy

FitFrame does not knowingly collect personal information from children because it does not collect user data.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
